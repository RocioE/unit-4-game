Player will be given random number at the start of The Dark Crystal. There are 4 crystals. When the player clicks on a crystal, it will add a specific amount of points to the player's total score. The player wins if their total score matches the random number given at the beginning of the game. The player loses if their total score goes above the random number given at the start of the game. The value of each crystal is hidden from the player until it is clicked.
